# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
A: Use the 'Forgot Password' link on the login page.

**Q: How do I upload a document?**
A: Go to the Documents section in your dashboard and click 'Upload Document.'

**Q: How do I join a virtual court session?**
A: Click 'Join Video Session' in the Sessions section of your dashboard.

**Q: Who do I contact for support?**
A: Use the support contact in the app or email support@example.com.

For more help, click the help icon (?) in the app.
